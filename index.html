Our Story

It all started with a passion for photography and boredom in a communication technologies classroom. Having earned all possible credits for that 10, 20, and 30 level class I was told to come up with my own project for extra credits. So, I found Blender and did my first ever ball bounce animation. From then I was nominated and received a scholarship to advance my education and now professional journey in the 3D world.


Throughout my training, I worked extensively with industry-standard tools including Nuke, Maya, and the Adobe Creative Suite, developing practical skills in compositing, 3D workflows, and post-production processes. This experience allowed me to build a solid understanding of how individual pipeline stages interconnect, as well as the importance of efficiency, consistency, and collaboration within a production environment. In addition to technical proficiency, my work has strengthened my problem-solving abilities, attention to detail, and capacity to iterate based on feedback—skills that are essential in fast-paced, production-driven settings. I approach visual effects with a balance of creativity and precision, ensuring that technical execution supports narrative and visual continuity.
